Welcome to Tom and Jerry game

A game about Tom and Jerry and Cheeses and my lack of sleep as well T.T

  1. Enter your name and pick a character as you want to be.
  2. As Jerry, your job is just to take many cheeses as you can and stay away from Tom. Each cheese will get you 1 point.
  3. As Tom, you know that it's already hard to be Tom. If Jerry gets 3 cheeses, you will lose. Your job is to chase away Jerry. (I will make Jerry run faster tho)
  4. Just press reset when ever the time is up.
